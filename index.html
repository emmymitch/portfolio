About.

Hi, I'm Emily! I'm a cheerful and adaptable junior software developer excited to get into the technology sector. Currently I'm based in Suffolk, UK, but am willing and eager to relocate.

Before this, I completed a masters' degree in physics at the University of Southampton. As part of this I learned to code in Python, which I greatly enjoyed - the sheer logic of it especially. I also volunteered at the student cinema, designing publicity and marketing materials. When I found _nology's course, it seemed like a great way to combine these two and do something I love.

And it was! Throughout the course I've learned many new skills and applied them to several projects which I'm proud of. You can have a look at them above. It's my desire to now take these skills futher and become a successful software developer.
